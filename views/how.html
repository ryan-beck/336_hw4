The Bitcoin network is powered by blockchain technology. Blockchain allows for a decentralized ledger, or record of all payments made on the system. All the money in circulation is accounted for and anyone can see any transaction. This makes fraud nearly impossible.

The network is supported by decentralized nodes which keep track of the ledger. Transactions on the network are supported by a process known as "mining". Bitcoin miners give their computing power to the network and are paid in Bitcoin, which is how new Bitcoin is created.
