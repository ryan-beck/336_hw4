The idea behind Bitoin was created by an unknown person, or persons, going by the alias of Satoshi Nakamoto. In October 2008, an article was published by Nakamoto: “A Peer-to-Peer Electronic Cash System”, which outlined the basis of the Bitcoin ecosystem.

The first version of Bitcoin code was released in January 2009. The first block in the Bitcoin blockchain was created. This transaction was between Nakamoto and one of the developers named Hal Finney.
