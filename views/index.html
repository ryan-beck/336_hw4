Bitcoin is a cryptocurrency, and can be considered to be the father of all cryptocurrencies. A cryptocurrency is a decentralized digital currency without a need for a central authority, such as a bank. It is sent from user to user with no need for a middle man, and is maintained by decentralized servers meaning it has no single authority that controls it.

Some people may not see Bitcoin in the best light, due to it's price volatility and association with crime. However, the technolgy behind it quite impressive and potentially world changing.
